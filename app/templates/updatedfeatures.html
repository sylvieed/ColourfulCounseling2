New Features

Sentiment Analysis

We've implemented a sentiment analysis tool that allows you to analyze your drawing's emotional tone. Our image object analysis will help you understand the objects and symbols you depict in your artwork.

Improved Upload Image and AI-Generated Drawing Prompts

We've also improved our upload image functionality, making it easier for you to share your creations. Our AI-generated drawing prompts will provide you with unique challenges, and we've redesigned our drawing prompt page for a more user-friendly experience. Our prompts are now based on your past drawings and emotions, giving you an opportunity to explore your inner self.

Exclusively Focused on Drawings and Photos

We are now exclusively focused on drawings and photography instead of sculpting and collaging.

Multi-Language Support

We have also added multi-language support, so everyone can access and benefit from our website.
